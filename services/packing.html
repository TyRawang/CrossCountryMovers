Cross Country Movers offers partial and full packing options for all of our customers. With partial packing, you can choose which items you would like to pack or not pack. Items that you wish for us to pack, we can pack at an additional cost on the day of the pickup. Our movers always come prepared for any of your packing needs. Customers can also choose our comprehensive full packing option which is a luxury service that covers all of your items from A-Z. All you would need to do is open the door and let us start. We bring all the boxes, blankets, plastic covers and tape rolls. We bring wardrobe boxes, picture boxes and dish packs for your kitchen. You can rest assured that all of your fragile and antiques will be safe with our full pack option!

Wrapping and padding is a free service that Cross Country Movers offers, and although this is a basic service offered by many moving companies, we make sure to stand out by providing high-quality heavy-duty blankets that can pad your hard surfaced furniture securely. This way, your furniture is safe from all kinds of chips and scratches.

Cross Country Movers offers thick high-quality poly plastic bags for your soft surfaced furniture(s) such as mattresses, box springs as well as your sofas. Our premium poly plastic is 5 mm thick and is reusable for up to 5 years. You can choose to purchase our covers for an additional cost - speak with a relocation consultant to get more details.
