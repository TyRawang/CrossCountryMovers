Home About Us

Cross Country Movers offers two options for storage. We offer indoor and outdoor storage.

Our indoor storage is for customers who also book their relocation with our company. The first four weeks are free of charge, and additional storage is charged on a monthly basis. Please call our offices and speak to a relocation consultant to inquire about monthly charges and charges relating to your relocation.

Our outdoor storage is offered to the public and uses self-storage containers. We have 20 foot and 40-foot containers that can store up to 10 000 LBS. Our yard is accessible 24/7 and we provide you with a gate access code. You can rest assured that your belongings are safe in the storage as it is 24-hour surveillance, gated and locked, and patrolled on the weekends.

Cross Country Movers is extremely flexible with storage and we have monthly deals for different customer needs! We offer storage options all over Canada! Please contact us to learn more and to develop a moving or storage plan for you.
